Why turkaffe?

The Norwegian turkaffe, literally hiking coffee, is one of the oldest coffee traditions. It is prepared simply but meticulously and that Kristen goes about her life. Deliberately creating space to function in a hectic world. She aims to bring that same meticulous approach to all she does.
